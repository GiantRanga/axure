Skål is founded. Thor opens a small workshop in his garage and hires some friends to create one of a kind furniture pieces for affluent members of his community.

2009

Skål was founded in 2009 after current managing director       Thor Jacobsson was laid off from his office job, a result of the 2008 global financial crisis. Now finding time to observe his home space, he felt there was a distinct lack of meaningfully designed and high quality furniture. Identifying a gap in the global furniture market, Thor started his company in his garage and has now grown to distribute his creations in 25 countries around the world.

ORIGINS

Skål

After growing his list of clientelle over two years, Thor opens the first Skål Lab, a modest studio in the heart of Västerås.

The team has since grown to 6 full time furniture artisans and a management team that handles administration.

2011

Following the continual growth of Skål and the opening of warehouses and workshops in all four major cities in Sweden, Skål was awarded the coveted ‘Cross of Sweden’ by the Swedish government.

2014

Skål has now entered the global market, with stores and logistic networks setup across Europe and the Americas. Keeping to it’s roots, all pieces continue to be hand crafted and are only available to purchase in store personally.

2018

Skål has recently launched a highly successful IPO on the Swedish stock exchange under the ticker: SKL

This marks a turning point for Swedes to be a part of the global furniture empire that is Skål.

2022

“At Skål, we approach the boundaries of design as hurdles. Things to leap over and keep going.”

- Thor Jacobsson, Founder
